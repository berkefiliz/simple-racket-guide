About the guide


What is this?

This guide is a simplified version of the official documentation, in the hopes to make learning Typed Racket easier. The courses are ordered in a way that makes sense if someone decides to learn Typed Racket themselves. I hope you find the guide useful!


How should I study?

The guide will provide basic examples to get you started on the language. However, it is crucial that you experiment with code yourself and read the official Racket documentation alongside. I will be adding some example questions and challenges here and there to encourage you reading further. If you have any questions, you can also contact me!


Acknowledgements

Even though I double check everything I write here, this does not mean it is correct. Indeed, I hope that the guide will aid the learning process; however I cannot guarantee that the information here is up-to-date. Moreover, this guide does not intend on helping people to master the language! It is a great way to start learning the language, but there is a lot more to the language. Once you get the hang of the structure and essentials of this language, I highly recommend reading other resources.
